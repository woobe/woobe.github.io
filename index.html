About Jo-fai Chow

I am currently a final year engineering doctorate candidate in hydroinformatics (water + machine learning). Previously I was a software technology consultant specialised in water infrastructure, data-driven modelling and large-scale optimisation.

At the moment, I am busy writing up my thesis but I still enjoy contributing to open-source projects in my spare time. I am particularly interested in data mining and visualisation projects using R, Python & MATLAB. Recently, I gave a talk on open data, visualisation and web app.
