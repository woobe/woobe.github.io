About Jo-fai Chow

Jo-fai is currently a final year engineering doctorate candidate in hydroinformatics (water + machine learning). His background is in water engineering, data-driven modelling and large-scale optimisation. He has been providing decision support technology to utilities sector since 2005.

At the moment, Jo-fai is busy writing up his thesis but he still finds time to contribute to open-source projects on GitHub. He is particularly interested in data mining and visualisation projects using R, Python & MATLAB. Recently, he gave a talk on open data, visualisation and web app development.
